What is CloudIT?

CloudIT abstracts the concept of hiring, making employers free to find the qualified professional for the execution of specific tasks.
Our platform is for freelancers and employers that show interest in working/outsourcing in the field of IT for particular projects.

Our Intent

The aim is to optimize the work-force hiring processes in the IT department and approximate companies and employees.
We distinguish ourselves from similar platforms on the market as we establish a direct connection between employer and freelancer, without complications or intermediary processes.
